Our Story

Soul from the Backwaters

Paint a picture of Kerala's backwaters arriving in the heart of Bengaluru. At Kaliyodam, we don't just serve meals; we share our heritage. Step onto our food boat and leave the city bustle behind.

We believe in the soul of Kerala cooking — slow fire, fresh coconut, and hand-ground spices passed down through generations. Every fish curry is simmered in earthen pots, and every sadya leaf holds the warmth of our grandmother's kitchen.
